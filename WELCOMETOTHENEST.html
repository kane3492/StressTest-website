Welcome To The Nest (Rehearsed Reading)
Written by Kane Heath
Directed by Lucy Sellers
2025 · Studio 5 - University Of Chichester

'Welcome To The Nest' was a staged reading of a new stage play inspired by Greek Mythology; acting as a practical proof of concept to experiment with contemporary materials. It played to two sold out audiences at the University Of Chichester. All performance photography by @createv.bell on Instagram.
