Ginger Beer vs. Dr. Stepper
Created by Kane Heath
and Charley Macfarlane
2026 · University Of Chichester

'Ginger Beer versus Dr Stepper' was a Theatre Politics and Activism piece parodying popular Horse-Racing events such as Goodwood to highlight the neglect and lack of knowledge surrounding the mistreating of animals within this cultural events. With event hostage by Matt Wason of BlueJeansTheatre Co. and performers Kane Heath and Charley MacFarlane.
