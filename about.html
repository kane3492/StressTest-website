Who We Are

StressTest is a West-Sussex based Arts 'Company' that exits to put new work to the test. We exist to develop, produce and stress test new theatre.

What We Do

StressTest exists as a label of sort for experimental work to wear, whether it be workshop, scratch work or fully-staged work That means rehearsed readings, workshops, scratch nights and fully staged shows; an opportunity to take risks and establish a name to experiment under.
